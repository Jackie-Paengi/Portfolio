About Lolkipayangi

Hello! I'm Jackson, I am a dynamic and results-oriented ICT Specialist with proven expertise in IT support, RFID asset tagging, tracking, and web design. With hands-on experience from roles at Eco-Oryx Solutions Limited and a prestigious internship at ICT Authority (Kenya Revenue Authority), I have honed my skills in delivering innovative technical solutions, improving operational efficiency, and providing user training and support. My technical strengths include troubleshooting, system maintenance, network infrastructure management, and deploying cutting-edge IT systems in various sectors. I am committed to continuous learning and am well-versed in leveraging technology to contribute to the success of forward-thinking teams.
